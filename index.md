**What is the most interesting topic covered in your domain this quarter?**  

The most interesting topic we covered in our meetings so far, to me, was catastrophe modeling.
When performing catastrophe modeling, there are four main steps: Hazard, Vulnerability, Exposure, and Loss. Step one, hazard, determine the hazard, which is the combination of event intensity, duration, and frequency of occurrence (IDF). Step two, vulnerability, establishes the impact the severity of the hazard will have on the natural or built environment based on how sensitive and vulnerable it is. Step three, exposure, get the location and type of asset to join with the local hazard and appropriate impact function. Step four, loss, compute the loss and damage metrics of an asset.
